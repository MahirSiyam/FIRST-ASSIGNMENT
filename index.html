Born on June 28, 1940, Yunus earned his Ph.D. in economics from Vanderbilt University in the United States. His innovative banking model provides small loans to impoverished entrepreneurs without requiring collateral, empowering them to start or grow businesses. This approach has significantly reduced poverty and improved living standards, especially for women, in many developing countries."
